About this work

This study proposes a geological model characterization framework that combines stratigraphic forward modeling with a single-image diffusion model, SinFusion, to generate multiple geologically realistic realizations while enabling flexible well data integration during reverse diffusion. The approach is designed to support efficient model updates and ensemble-based uncertainty assessment in industrial-scale workflows.
